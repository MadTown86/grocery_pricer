Purpose:
This document outlines html structure of chosen websites and under which html tags information is located.  

Scope:

    www.target.com
    www.walmart.com
    www.heinens.com

    Information:

Future Statement:
Defining specific html structure to find information is restrictive and not future proof as websites are living.  A more robust application could outline a list of key words and primarily
search inner text for keywords and structure data recursively.
